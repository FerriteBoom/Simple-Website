Welcome to Sachi Salon

At Sachi Salon, we are passionate about bringing your beauty aspirations to life. Located in the heart of Kingswood, New South Wales, Australia, our salon is a haven of relaxation and style. We combine traditional techniques with modern trends to offer a diverse range of services, all delivered by our highly skilled and friendly team.
